About Myself

I am a person who is positive about every aspect of life. There are many things I like to do, to see and to experience. I like to read; I like to write; I like to think; I like to dream; I like to talk; I like to be listened; I like to listen too. I like to see the sunrise in the morning; I like to see the moonlight at night; I like to smell the wind; I like to look at the clouds in the sky; I like to observe emptiness of the sky and want to be empty. I like to visit the beautiful places in Nepal; I like country’s peace; I like nature; I like flowers in spring, rain in summer, leaves in autumn, and snow in winter; I like the land and the nature, I like people. And, I like to laugh. I like delicious vegan food; I like good books and romantic movies. I am just someone who does some experiments. I’m someone who wants to know about MY SELF. I’m someone who wants to know “Where we came from & where will we go?” I’m someone who is searching “Failure in the success and achievement in the failure.” This is a brief introduction of me; But in actual I don’t know about myself. “My life is in search of myself.”
